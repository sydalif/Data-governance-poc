Annual Data Governance Briefing (Singapore Public Agency)

As an agency responsible for the registration and regulation of businesses in Singapore, we are entrusted with handling sensitive personal and commercial information.

Data governance ensures we manage, protect, and use this data ethically, securely, and lawfully, in accordance with the **Public Sector (Governance) Act**, **PDPA**, and internal policies.

  • Ensure accurate, complete and timely recording of registration data
  • Respect data confidentiality and prevent unauthorized sharing
  • Report data breaches or suspicious access immediately
  • Only access records that are necessary for your role
